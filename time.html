Time converter

Definition

Time can be described as the distance between two different events. Our whole life is built around the concept of time, yet it is almost impossible to define time in logical terms without assuming that it is already defined. In the same way length defines the distance between two points, the passage of time can be observed and measured with the changes of movement and aging for example. Time is often referred to as the fourth dimension, along with the spatial dimensions.
